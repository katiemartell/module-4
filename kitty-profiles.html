Welcome to our Kitty Profiles!

Thank you for visiting our site today. We would like to highligh several of our featured kitties that are ready to rent. We hope that you find them as purr-fect as we do!

Nutella

Nutella is a friendly feline. She is a 5 year old gray main coon who loves to cuddle and sit by the fire. She loves attention and will always be by your side! Nutella would be perfect for a home with adults. She doesn't play well with children and other animals.

Bailey

Bailey is a 10 month old kitten. She is full of energy and is constantly on the move! Bailey would be great with children and families who love to play with their animals. Bailey doesn't mind being around other cats as long as she can be the center of attention!

Buttercup

Buttercup is a 8 year old tabby cat. She has the strongest personality of all the cats avaliable. She can be affectionate at times, but prefers to be on her own. She would not do well with childen. We found she prefers single person households where she becomes queen of the home.
